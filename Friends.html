ABOUT

Friends is an American television sitcom created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, which aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004, lasting ten seasons. With an ensemble cast starring Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc, Matthew Perry, and David Schwimmer, the show revolves around six friends in their 20s and 30s who live in Manhattan, New York City. The series was produced by Bright/Kauffman/Crane Productions, in association with Warner Bros. Television. Friends received acclaim throughout its run, becoming one of the most popular television shows of all time. It was nominated for 62 Primetime Emmy Awards, winning the Outstanding Comedy Series award in 2002 for its eighth season. The series has ranked on various TV guides' lists and maintains a strong following even after its original broadcast ended, largely due to its memorable characters, comedic moments, and cultural impact.

PLOT

The series follows the personal and professional lives of six friends in their 20s and 30s who live in Manhattan, New York City. The core group consists of: **Rachel Green**: A fashion enthusiast and Monica's best friend from childhood. Rachel starts the series by running away from her wedding and moving in with Monica. She begins working as a waitress at Central Perk, the group's favorite coffee shop, and eventually pursues a career in fashion, working her way up from a personal shopper at Bloomingdale's to an executive at Ralph Lauren. **Monica Geller**: A chef known for her obsessive cleanliness and competitive nature. Monica is Ross's younger sister and lives in an apartment inherited from her grandmother. Throughout the series, she strives to establish herself as a chef and eventually opens her own restaurant. Monica's romantic relationships, including her eventual marriage to Chandler, are central to her storyline. **Phoebe Buffay**: An eccentric masseuse and musician. Known for her quirky behavior and odd beliefs, Phoebe has a rough past but remains optimistic and kind-hearted. She performs original songs at Central Perk and pursues various jobs throughout the series. Phoebe's offbeat humor and unique outlook on life make her one of the show's standout characters. **Joey Tribbiani**: A struggling actor and food lover who becomes known for his role on the soap opera "Days of Our Lives" as Dr. Drake Ramoray. Joey is known for his simple-minded but good-hearted nature and his many romantic escapades. His catchphrase "How you doin'?" becomes iconic. Joey's acting career, friendships, and his relationship with his roommate Chandler form key parts of his storyline. **Chandler Bing**: Known for his sarcastic sense of humor, Chandler works in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration before moving into advertising. His childhood trauma, including his parents' divorce and his father's drag show, are often sources of his humor and insecurities. Chandler's close friendship with Joey, and later his relationship and marriage to Monica, are central to his character development. **Ross Geller**: A paleontologist and Monica's older brother. Ross is known for his intellectual demeanor and a series of divorces. His romantic feelings for Rachel are a major storyline throughout the series, leading to their on-again, off-again relationship. Ross also deals with the challenges of co-parenting his son, Ben, and later his daughter, Emma, with Rachel. The group navigates the trials and tribulations of careers, romantic relationships, and friendships. Key plotlines include Ross and Rachel's tumultuous relationship, Monica and Chandler's evolving romance from friends to married couple, Joey's acting career, and Phoebe's search for family and love.
